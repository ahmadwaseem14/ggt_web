Customer Support

Need help? Find the best way to reach us and get assistance with your PC hardware needs.

How Can We Help?

Our team is here to assist you with various inquiries. We can help with:

  • Pre-sales questions about products and compatibility.
  • Checking current stock status and pricing (via WhatsApp/Phone).
  • Guidance on placing an order.
  • Updates on an existing order or delivery status.
  • Basic technical support or troubleshooting advice.
  • Warranty information and assistance with claim processes.
  • Inquiries about custom PC builds.

Quickest Response for Pricing & Orders: For the fastest response regarding product prices, availability, and placing orders, please contact us directly via WhatsApp during business hours.

Information for Faster Support

To help us assist you more efficiently, please have the following information ready when you contact us (if applicable):

  • Your Name
  • Order Number (for existing orders)
  • Product Name / SKU (for product inquiries)
  • A clear description of your question or the issue you are facing
  • Screenshots or photos (if relevant to the issue)

Response Times & Availability

Our primary support hours are 10:00 AM - 10:00 PM, Monday to Sunday. We strive to respond to WhatsApp messages and phone calls promptly during these hours. Email inquiries may take 1-2 business days for a detailed response. Support is limited outside of business hours and on public holidays.
